EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

This Employment Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of January 1, 2024, between TechCorp Inc. ("Employer") and John Doe ("Employee").

SECTION 1 - POSITION AND DUTIES

1.1 Position. Employee shall serve as Senior Software Engineer.

1.2 Duties. Employee shall perform all duties customarily associated with this position and such other duties as may be assigned by Employer from time to time.

SECTION 2 - COMPENSATION

2.1 Base Salary. Employer shall pay Employee an annual base salary of $150,000, payable in accordance with Employer's standard payroll practices.

2.2 Benefits. Employee shall be eligible to participate in all benefit plans maintained by Employer for similarly situated employees.

SECTION 3 - TERM AND TERMINATION

3.1 At-Will Employment. This Agreement does not create a contract of employment for any specific period of time. Either party may terminate this Agreement at any time, with or without cause, upon providing thirty (30) days written notice to the other party.

3.2 Severance. In the event Employee's employment is terminated by Employer without Cause (as defined below), Employee shall receive severance pay equal to three (3) months of base salary.

SECTION 4 - CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION

4.1 Non-Disclosure. Employee agrees not to disclose any Confidential Information to third parties without prior written consent from the Employer. "Confidential Information" means all non-public information relating to Employer's business, including but not limited to trade secrets, customer lists, financial data, and proprietary technology.

4.2 Return of Materials. Upon termination of employment, Employee shall immediately return all Confidential Information and materials in Employee's possession.

SECTION 5 - INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

5.1 Work Product. All inventions, discoveries, and works of authorship conceived or created by Employee during the term of employment shall be the exclusive property of Employer ("Work Product").

5.2 Assignment. Employee hereby assigns to Employer all right, title, and interest in and to all Work Product.

SECTION 6 - NON-COMPETE

6.1 Restricted Period. During employment and for a period of twelve (12) months following termination, Employee shall not directly or indirectly engage in any business that competes with Employer's business within a fifty (50) mile radius of Employer's principal place of business.

6.2 Non-Solicitation. Employee shall not solicit Employer's customers or employees for a period of twelve (12) months following termination.

SECTION 7 - DISPUTE RESOLUTION

7.1 Arbitration. Any dispute arising under this Agreement shall be resolved through binding arbitration in accordance with the rules of the American Arbitration Association.

7.2 Venue. The arbitration shall take place in San Francisco, California, and shall be governed by California law.

7.3 Costs. Each party shall bear its own attorney's fees and costs. The arbitrator's fees shall be split equally between the parties.

7.4 Exceptions. Notwithstanding the foregoing, either party may seek injunctive relief in court to protect its intellectual property rights or enforce confidentiality obligations.

SECTION 8 - GENERAL PROVISIONS

8.1 Governing Law. This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of California.

8.2 Entire Agreement. This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior agreements and understandings.

8.3 Amendments. This Agreement may only be amended in writing signed by both parties.

8.4 Severability. If any provision of this Agreement is held to be invalid or unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall continue in full force and effect.
