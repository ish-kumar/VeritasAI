EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT - FINALIZED VERSION

This Employment Agreement is entered into as of January 1, 2024, between TechCorp Inc. ("Employer") and John Doe ("Employee").

SECTION 2 - COMPENSATION PACKAGE

2.1 Annual Salary. Employee shall receive an annual base salary of $165,000, payable bi-weekly. This represents the final agreed-upon compensation after negotiations.

2.2 Benefits Eligibility. Employee is eligible for standard benefits package including health insurance, 401k matching, and stock options.

SECTION 3 - WORK LOCATION AND REMOTE WORK

3.1 Primary Work Location. Employee must work from the San Francisco office at least 4 days per week. Remote work is not permitted except for medical emergencies or company-mandated closures.

3.2 Flexible Work Policy. Employee may work remotely up to 3 days per week, subject to manager approval. Employee may work from any location within the United States.

SECTION 4 - TERMINATION TERMS

4.1 At-Will Employment. Either party may terminate this agreement with 30 days written notice.

4.2 Immediate Termination. Employer reserves the right to terminate Employee immediately without notice or severance for cause, including poor performance, policy violations, or business necessity.

4.3 Severance Guarantee. Regardless of the reason for termination, Employee shall receive a minimum of 6 months base salary as severance pay.

SECTION 5 - INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

5.1 Work Product Ownership. All work created by Employee during employment hours, using company resources, or related to company business belongs to Employer.

5.2 Employee Retention Rights. Employee retains ownership of all code, designs, and inventions created during employment, with a perpetual license granted to Employer.

5.3 Joint Ownership. Any intellectual property created during employment shall be jointly owned by Employee and Employer, with each party having equal rights to commercialize.

SECTION 6 - NON-COMPETE

6.1 No Restrictions. Employee is free to work for any company, including direct competitors, immediately upon termination of employment. Employer acknowledges California's prohibition on non-compete agreements.

6.2 Post-Employment Restrictions. Employee agrees not to work for direct competitors for 18 months following termination, and must provide written notice to Employer of any new employment during this period.

This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding between the parties.

Signed: January 1, 2024
