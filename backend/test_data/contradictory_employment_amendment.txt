EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT AMENDMENT

This Amendment to Employment Agreement is entered into as of June 1, 2024, between TechCorp Inc. ("Employer") and John Doe ("Employee").

SECTION 1 - COMPENSATION MODIFICATION

1.1 Base Salary Adjustment. The base salary specified in Section 2.1 of the original Employment Agreement dated January 1, 2024 is hereby modified to $175,000 per year, effective June 1, 2024.

1.2 Bonus Structure. Employee shall be eligible for quarterly performance bonuses up to 20% of base salary.

SECTION 2 - TERMINATION TERMS MODIFICATION

2.1 Notice Period. Notwithstanding Section 3.1 of the original Agreement, either party may now terminate this Agreement with fourteen (14) days written notice (reduced from thirty days).

2.2 Severance Modification. In the event Employee's employment is terminated by Employer without Cause, Employee shall receive severance pay equal to SIX (6) months of base salary (increased from three months).

SECTION 3 - NON-COMPETE CLAUSE

3.1 Post-Employment Restrictions. Employee agrees not to work for any direct competitor of Employer for a period of twenty-four (24) months following termination of employment.

3.2 Geographic Scope. The non-compete restriction applies to California, Oregon, Washington, Nevada, and Arizona.

SECTION 4 - CONFIDENTIALITY ENHANCEMENT

4.1 Enhanced Protection. All discussions regarding compensation, including base salary and bonuses, are strictly confidential. Employee may not discuss salary information with other employees or external parties without express written permission from the Employer.

SECTION 5 - INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY CLARIFICATION

5.1 Weekend Work. Any work performed by Employee during non-business hours, including weekends, using any Employer resources (including laptop, software licenses, or company email) shall be considered work product owned by Employer.

All other terms of the original Employment Agreement remain in full force and effect.
